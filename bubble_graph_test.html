Bubble Graph Test

Bubble Graph is a wrapper for RaphealJS that makes creating a bubble chart a little easier. Currently alpha. ;)

Personal skill assessment, 2013

  • The line thickness represents my skill level.
  • The distance between me and the subject represents my interest level.
  • The size of the circle represents how much time I get to spend on that subject.
  • Brown are client side languages, blue are server side languages, purple are DB languages (ish).

Code:
